# Revenue Funnel Model

## Default Funnel

Konten gratis -> Newsletter/Substack -> Entry Product -> Membership

## KPI Funnel

| Stage | KPI | Catatan |
|-------|-----|---------|
| Audience | Traffic, views, subscriber growth | Ukur kemampuan konten menarik perhatian |
| Lead | Email subscriber, waitlist | Ukur trust dan minat awal |
| Buyer | Penjualan entry product | Ukur product-market signal |
| Member | Active member, churn, retention | Ukur kualitas value yang berulang |

## Eksperimen Awal

- Tambahkan CTA waitlist di setiap artikel pilar.
- Tambahkan CTA newsletter di setiap video edukasi.
- Uji lead magnet sederhana: checklist valuasi atau template watchlist.
